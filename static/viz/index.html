Visualization Two

Sentiment Analysis

Compares the sentiment analysis of Credible and Not-Credible articles of the corpus. The bar graph shows the average percentage of positive, negative and neutral number of words in the articles, the graph shows that Not Credible articles on average have more positive and negative words but less neutral words.

The line graph shows the distribution of the compound values for Credible and Not-Credible articles, the distribution shows that the percentage of Credible articles with compound around zero is higher than Not-Credible articles.
